About Us

Our Mission

Here at Snooky's Cookies we have always had one simple goal in mind: Bringing you delicious, mouth watering, freshly baked cookies. Whether we deliver them straight to your doorstep, or hand them to you in store, all we want is for you to enjoy them.

Our Story

It all started back in 1938 during the Great Depression. In the midst of terrible times for the country, one young baker from Boston Massachusetts had a dream. Papa Snooky, as he came to be known, started baking cookies out of his Medford home, just to give his neighbors. He didn't ask for any money. He didn't even think of it as a business. All he wanted was to spread joy to those who seldom saw it, through his delicious cookies. To the lucky few in Papa's Medford neighborhood who ate his cookies, they were more than just delicious, they were a beam of light in dark times. A beam of light that they felt needed to be spread beyond Medford. And so, with the help of a community, the business was born. In 1939, Papa opened up the first ever Snooky's Cookies in Davis Square, in the neighboring town of Somerville. In the economic downturn, business was slow at first, but nothing would keep those cookies down. In 1941, after the depression ended Papa's son, Calvin, was able to open a second location in Boston's North End, which went on to become the most popular cookie bakery in the country. The rest, as they say, is history.
